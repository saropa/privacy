Data Deletion Instructions for Saropa Contacts

All user data for the Saropa Contacts application is stored locally on your device. We do not store your data on any remote servers. To permanently delete your data, you must clear the application's data or uninstall the app from your device.

Instructions for iOS (iPhone/iPad)

  1. Find the Saropa Contacts app icon on your Home Screen.
  2. Press and hold the app icon until a menu appears.
  3. Tap Remove App, then confirm by tapping Delete App. This will permanently delete the application and all of its data from your device.

Instructions for Android

  1. Open your device's Settings app.
  2. Navigate to Apps > See all apps and find Saropa Contacts in the list.
  3. Tap on the app to open its app info page.
  4. On the app info page, tap on Storage & cache.
  5. Tap Clear storage to permanently delete all app data. Alternatively, you can uninstall the app.
